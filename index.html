CKA-RL: Continual Knowledge Adaptation for Reinforcement Learning
Jinwu Hu*, Zihao Lian* (co-first author), Zhiquan Wen, Chenghao Li, Guohao Chen, Xutao Wen, Bin Xiao, Mingkui Tan
NeurIPS, 2025
code / paper

We propose CKA-RL, a Continual Reinforcement Learning framework that enables efficient knowledge accumulation and transfer across tasks through a knowledge vector pool and an adaptive knowledge merging mechanism. CKA-RL alleviates catastrophic forgetting and improves forward transfer by 4.20%, demonstrating strong adaptability in non-stationary environments.
